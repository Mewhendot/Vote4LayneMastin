About Me

WHO IS
LAYNE MASTIN

★★★

Hi, I'm Layne Mastin — a dedicated student at Bracken County High School and proud four-year member of Future Business Leaders of America. I maintain a 4.0 GPA and have completed both the Accounting Pathway and the Business Achievement Awards (BAA), building a strong foundation in financial responsibility, organization, and principled leadership.

Through FBLA, I have qualified for state in Intro to Business Concepts and Human Resources — experiences that have deepened my understanding of business competition and high-level preparation. From regional competitions to the NFLC conference, I've been in the room and I know what it takes.

Outside of school, I stay grounded through fishing, music, and basketball — all activities that have built my patience, teamwork, and drive. As your State Treasurer, I'll bring those same qualities to every financial decision I make on behalf of Kentucky FBLA members statewide.
